Best Areas in Rio

The best area depends on the visitor: first-time tourist, premium traveler, executive, event guest or group of friends.

COPA

Copacabana

Practical, famous and hotel-rich. Strong for first-time visitors who want beach access, restaurants and recognizable surroundings.

IPA

Ipanema

Stylish, social and refined. A strong choice for visitors who want beach culture, dining, bars and a more polished atmosphere.

LEB

Leblon

Upscale, calm and premium. Best for travelers who want discretion, restaurants, comfort and a more exclusive Rio rhythm.

BAR

Barra da Tijuca

Modern, spread out and useful for events, longer stays, business logistics and larger hotel properties.

CTR

Centro and Porto Maravilha

Useful for business, events and culture. Better with a clear purpose and more attention to movement at night.
