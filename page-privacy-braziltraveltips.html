Privacy policy

Clear guidance deserves clear privacy.

This policy explains how Brazil Travel Tips should handle visitor information, editorial contact, analytics, external links and privacy-sensitive travel topics.

Contact us Editorial policy
Short answer Brazil Travel Tips is designed to be privacy-aware. The site should collect only what is needed for basic analytics, site operation, voluntary contact and functional preferences such as adult-age confirmation for restricted Premium Services areas.

Information We May Collect

When the site is live, Brazil Travel Tips may collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited and referral source through privacy-conscious analytics tools. If a visitor contacts us, we may receive the information they choose to provide, such as name, email address and message content.

We should not ask visitors for sensitive personal details unless there is a clear, necessary and lawful reason.

How Information Is Used

Improve Content

Analytics can help us understand which guides, cities, languages and travel topics are most useful.

Respond to Contact

Information sent through contact channels should be used to reply to the request and manage relevant communication.

Protect Trust

We may use technical signals to reduce spam, abuse, fraud or misuse of contact forms and site features.

External Links

Brazil Travel Tips may link to external resources, including travel resources, city references and discreet premium-service partners. External websites have their own privacy practices. Visitors should review those policies before sharing personal information elsewhere.

A link from Brazil Travel Tips does not mean an official partnership with hotels, public agencies or tourism authorities unless that relationship is clearly stated.

Age Confirmation for Premium Services

Some Premium Services areas may include discreet information intended only for adults, such as private concierge-style guidance, nightlife planning or links to adult-oriented external resources. Before accessing those areas, visitors may be asked to confirm that they are 18 years or older and legally allowed to view that content in their jurisdiction.

This confirmation is declaratory. Brazil Travel Tips does not ask visitors to upload identity documents, passports, selfies, CPF numbers, full birth dates, proof of address or biometric information for this age confirmation.

If a visitor confirms adult age, the site may store a functional preference in the browser, such as a cookie or localStorage value, so the confirmation does not need to be repeated on every visit. The planned preference name is btt_age_confirmed, with a recommended duration of 30 days. This preference should not contain a name, document number, birth date or other sensitive identity data.

General travel guides, city pages, neighborhood maps, hotel guidance, restaurant tips, transport information, safety content and editorial policies should remain available without age confirmation unless they directly open restricted Premium Services content or adult-oriented external links.

Sensitive Topics

Some pages discuss private concierge, nightlife planning or companionship-related resources for adult visitors. These topics should be treated with privacy, adult consent and professional standards in mind. We do not knowingly support content involving minors, coercion, trafficking, non-consent or illegal activity.

This policy is an operational draft for publication and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before the site goes live.
